01 / Ageing planet series
The paintings in this series reflect the effects of negative activities on our environment and the earth as a unit. The topics of Climate change, global warming, Poaching, Uncontrolled deforestation and other related issuesare not really taking seriously in many parts of the world but yet it’s evident that the negative effect of these factors are visible in flooded apartments and cities, rising sea levels in coastal regions, extinction of animals due to uncontrolled poaching and many more.

02 /Depth Of Solitude
In this project I explore conversations and/or interactions that take place between individuals and their inner self in certain Life situations. These conversations are seen in the interactions between muscular realistic human Figures and the Hybrid black figures. These interactions take place in surrealistic spaces which represent the human mind. Depth of Solitude is inspired by the story of an Individual who was psychologically crushed and pushed to the end of depression but found his way to Strength and Recovery.

03 / Myth-series
This series presents a new body of mixed-media artworks which are not only visually arresting, but also serve as important artistic documentation of a culture at risk of being erased from public consciousness. The history of the Igbo people of South Eastern Nigeria dates back to the 9th century, but their indigenous way of life is rapidly fading in the face of dominating Western cultural narratives.Black figures with striking motifs painted white onto their skin appear against burning blue skies and stylised settings......
